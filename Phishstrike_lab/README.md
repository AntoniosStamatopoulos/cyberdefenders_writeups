# PhishStrike Lab – CyberDefenders

## Overview

This lab focuses on analyzing a phishing email campaign targeting an educational institution. The objective is to investigate suspicious email activity and understand how attackers deliver and execute malware through social engineering techniques.

The scenario simulates a real-world incident where a user receives a fraudulent email containing a malicious link disguised as an invoice. The analysis includes examining email headers, extracting indicators of compromise (IOCs), and investigating malicious infrastructure.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze phishing emails and identify suspicious indicators
* Examine email headers (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
* Extract and validate Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)
* Investigate malicious URLs and payload delivery
* Understand malware behavior and persistence mechanisms
* Identify command and control (C2) communication
* Analyze data exfiltration techniques
